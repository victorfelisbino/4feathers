How We Can Help 4 Feathers Grow

  1. Website & Digital Presence
    • Launch a modern, mobile-friendly website with strong calls-to-action and a gallery of real work.
    • Add a Google Business Profile and encourage customer reviews.
    • Share project photos and testimonials on Facebook and local groups.
    • Use local SEO keywords (e.g., “fence installation [your town]”).
  2. Direct Outreach
    • Design a simple, eye-catching letter or pamphlet offering free estimates and a special offer.
    • Mail or hand-deliver to homes and farms within a 10–20 mile radius.
    • Include a QR code or link to the website for easy contact.
  3. Government & Commercial Work
    • Register on local government and school district vendor lists (search for “procurement” or “vendor registration” for your county/city).
    • Check sites like SAM.gov for federal contracts, and local city/county bid boards for fencing jobs.
    • Network with local builders, realtors, and property managers.
  4. Scaling Operations
    • Collect and showcase more before/after photos and testimonials.
    • Consider hiring part-time help for installs or admin as leads grow.
    • Track all leads and follow up quickly.

Next Steps

  • Draft a sample letter/pamphlet for direct outreach.
  • Register for local government and school district vendor lists.
  • Continue collecting photos and testimonials for the website and social media.

This plan is designed to help Dalton and 4 Feathers Fencing & Contracting generate more leads, win more jobs, and grow sustainably. Please review and let us know your thoughts!
