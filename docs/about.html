Our Story

10+ Years of Building Fences & Relationships

4 Feathers Fencing & Contracting is a local, family-owned business based in Blanchard, Oklahoma. Founded by Dalton, we've spent over a decade delivering quality fencing, deck building, and contracting services to homeowners and businesses across the OKC metro area.

We believe every project deserves the same level of care and craftsmanship, whether it's a quick fence repair or a full custom installation. That's why our customers keep coming back — and keep recommending us to their neighbors.
